Sasha is a Product Designer based in Toronto with a background in economics and strategy. She designs intelligent, data-intensive systems that translate complex AI capabilities into intuitive, production-ready experiences — making them clear, trustworthy, and enjoyable.

At Boosted.ai, she designed AI tools for investment teams overseeing $7T+ in AUM, led end-to-end design of AI Chat & Voice experiences, and re-architected core workflows for portfolio managers and quant researchers operating in complex financial systems. She drove +34% growth in product adoption.
